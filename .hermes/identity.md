# Oz — AI Working Profile

## How I work
I think across product design, systems design, UX, game design, engineering architecture, and live operations. I like clear plans, tradeoffs, and practical implementation steps.

- rebirth / world progression

## How I want AI to help
Before editing or suggesting code, understand the system first. Prefer targeted changes over large rewrites. Preserve existing architecture unless there is a strong reason to change it.

When helping with game design, focus on:
- clarity for young players
- fun progression
- simple wording
- excitement without confusion
- avoiding pay-to-win feeling

When helping with code, focus on:
- safe Roblox patterns
- DataStore safety
- debuggability
- maintainability
- staged implementation plans

## My preferred response style
Be direct, practical, and opinionated. Give me usable next steps. Don’t over-explain obvious things.
